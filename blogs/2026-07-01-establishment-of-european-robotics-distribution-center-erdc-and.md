YASKAWA Electric Corporation has established a European Robotics Distribution Center in Kocevje, Slovenia, and relocated YASKAWA Ristro d.o.o. to the same site with roughly 5 billion yen invested. The expansion raises manipulator production capacity to 850 units and integrates development, production, and system functions to better support automotive, construction, and agricultural machinery customers.
